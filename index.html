Stacked to Semi-Colon

  1. Copy and paste a stacked Email Address List into the translator below.
  2. The Email Address List will automatically convert into a semi-colon seperated list.

Copy and paste a stacked list:

Warning! Please enter some text.
Success! Saved to clipboard.

stc1010@plymouth.edu; mcosma@plymouth.edu; test@test.edu; ed@gmail.com

mcosma@plymouth.edu
test@test.edu
ed@gmail.com

Comma to Stacked List

  1. Copy and paste a comma or semi-colon seperated Email Address List into the translator below.
  2. The Email Address List will automatically convert into a staced list.

Copy and paste a comma separated list:

Warning! Please enter some text.
Success! Saved to clipboard.

stc1010@plymouth.edu
mcosma@plymouth.edu
test@test.edu
ed@gmail.com

stc1010@plymouth.edu, mcosma@plymouth.edu, test@test.edu, ed@gmail.com

IMPORT A CONTACT LIST INTO O365

  1. Download and open the 0365 Contact Template.

  2. Copy and paste a stacked email address list under the Email Address List heading.

  3. Save the .csv file, then upload the .csv file into your Office 365 Contacts.
